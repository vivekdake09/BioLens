# BioLens
1.Biolens: Early Disease Symptom Checker with Privacy Focus (Healthcare Accessibility) Problem Solved: Delayed diagnosis in underserved areas due to lack of doctors or awareness. Unique Ideation: Chatbot + image upload (e.g., skin rash photos) for symptom analysis, risk scoring, and doctor referral suggestions. Emphasizes privacy (on-device processing). Tech Stack: ML: Fine-tuned LLM (e.g., BioBERT) for symptoms; CNN for skin conditions (DermNet dataset). Frontend: Gradio chat interface. Backend: FastAPI; optional AWS SageMaker endpoints. Why It Wins Innovation Track: Innovation (10): Hybrid text/image + ethical disclaimers. Technical Complexity (10): Medical NLP + CV. UI/UX (10): Conversational and reassuring. Usefulness/Impact (10): Early detection in remote areas. Business Value (10): Telemedicine integration. Kiro Integration: Deep planning for ethical AI (bias, accuracy) and crisis handling docs.
